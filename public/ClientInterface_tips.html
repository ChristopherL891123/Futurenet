Advice for Choosing the Right Model

Visualize your goal

- If you would like to categorize your data use a classifier model.

- If you would like to predict a value use a regression model.

- If you would like to group together observations and draw conclusions from them use a clustering model.

Understand your dataset

- All models automatically clean for NaN values.

- This project is more suited for small to medium dataset sizes. Expect performance drop when it comes to a larger dataset; our experts are currently upscaling our infrastructure.

- Numbers-based datasets are best for regression.

- Category number-based datasets are best for classifiers or clustering; it is important to have a goal in mind before choosing one!

Experiment your Ideas!

- There are many models to use for your own Machine Learning projects, so feel free to experiment all you like until you find a model that suits your needs.

- Feel free to experiment your ideas through our service!

- As of yet, there is no limit to model requests per user. Expect this to change soon.
